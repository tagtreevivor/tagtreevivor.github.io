# F11 - AMONG US CHALLENGE
4:01 PM
4:02 PM
Here's how it works! We're going to simulate 5 separate games of among us at once. And each game will have 2 impostors, randomly chosen, with identities only known to each other. Meaning that all of you (with one exception) will be the impostor in exactly 1 game, and crewmate in the other 4. (1 person will be a crewmate in all 5 games) This challenge will take place over 2 phases, each 12 hours long. (edited)
4:03 PM
Each phase you will perform an action in each of the 5 games, depending on whether you are a crewmate or an impostor in that game!
4:04 PM

PHASE 1

Crewmate Submit a vote for someone who they believe is the impostor. Choose a location to guard: The options include: Electrical, Reactor, Communications, Med-Bay, and Navigation. Impostor Select a player you would like to kill! They will not be able to participate in phase 2 Submit a vote for someone to be eliminated
4:05 PM
Only vote counts, and who is killed will be revealed publicly for each game. The players who are killed along with the player who receive the most votes will not be allowed to participate in phase two for those games. (edited)
4:06 PM

PHASE 2

Crewmate Submit a vote for someone who they believe is the impostor. Impostor Choose one location to sabotage! (same options as crewmate to guard) Select a player you would like to kill! Submit a vote for someone who you would like to see eliminated. (edited)
4:08 PM
And the end of phase 2: 1. The votes will be revealed, and the person voted out is eliminated from the game 2. If the impostor has not been voted out, they will try to sabotage on of the locations. The sabotage will succeed if no crewmates are guarding the area. 3. If the impostor is still alive, their final kill goes through. (edited)
4:09 PM
Points will then be tallied up and annouced
4:09 PM
Points are given out as follows for each phase for each game (edited)
4:10 PM
Phase 1: Crewmate receives 2 points if they vote for the impostor Crewmate receives 5 points if they are not killed/voted out Impostor receives 4 points if they are not voted out (edited)
4:13 PM
Phase 2: Crewmate receives 3 points if they vote for the impostor Crewmate receives 7 points if they are not killed/voted out Impostor receives 6 points if they are not voted out (edited)
4:18 PM
After Phase 2: If 2 impostors are alive, both receive 20 points. If 0 impostors are alive, all living crewmates receive 12 points If 1 impostor is alive living crewmates receive 5 points If the sabotage took place successfully, the living impostor(s) receive 30 points (edited)
4:19 PM
As an example, I may receive this in my submissions as my assignment: Game 1: Crewmate Game 2: Crewmate Game 3: Impostor with Austin Game 4: Crewmate Game 5: Crewmate My phase one submissions could then look like Game 1: Arvid, Electrical Game 2: Austin, Med-Bay Game 3: Vote: Arvid Kill: Muski Game 4: Quack, Reactor Game 5: Cwis, Reactor And assuming I survive every game, my phase two submission could look like Game 1: Cwis Game 2: Cwis Game 3: Vote Cwis Kill: Quack Sabotage: Electrical Game 4: Austin Game 5: Arvid (edited)
